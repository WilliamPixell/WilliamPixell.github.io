Main Strategies  
Fly-hover-ﬂy Strategy  
The ﬂy-hover-ﬂy strategy is described as  
UAVs ﬂy to the hovering location.  
UAVs hover over that location.  
UAVs return to the original position from the hovering location.  
Assumption 1  
The whole time horizon T is much longer than the time UAVs need to ﬂy  
to the hovering location.
